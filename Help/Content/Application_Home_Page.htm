Okta Application Page

The Applications page contains options that allow you to assign applications (apps) to users individually or through the "bulk" assignment of an app(s).

  • Top buttons allow you to easily add and assign applications for your end users.
  • Click the Active and Inactive tabs to display lists of your org's active and inactive apps, respectively.
  • Use the Active drop-down button menu adjacent to each app to easily activate or deactivate an active app. You can also delete inactive apps.
  • Use the Show app embed links button to link to an app outside of Okta.
  • Use the Refresh Application Data button to sync roles, profiles, and groups from configured apps into Okta with provisioning data.

  2. Use the alphabetical navigation bar and the search and filtering tools to look for the pre-integrated app you wish to add to your org.

Assign an Application

Assigning Individual Applications:

  1. From the Applications page, select an app you want to assign to one or more people or groups.
  2. Select the People tab to assign to individuals; click the Group tab, to assign to groups.
  3. From the app's details page, click the Assign to People / Assign to Groups button. An Assign <app name> to People / Assign <app name> to Groups dialog appears, listing available end users or groups who are not already assigned to the selected app.
  1. Click the Assign button next to each user or group you want this app assigned to.
  2. Click Done.

Bulk Assignment:

  1. From the Applications page, click Assign Applications.
  2. The Assign Applications page appears. On the left of this screen is a list of available apps. On the right of the screen, there is a list of people in your org.
  3. From the list of available apps, select the apps(s) that you want to assign to users. Selecting the check box at the top of the list selects all listed apps.
  4. From the list of people in your org, select the users to whom you want to assign the selected apps(s).
  5. Selecting the check box at the top of the list assigns the apps to all users. Alternatively, you can search by group:
  1. Select Search by group in the drop-down menu next to the People search field.
  2. Enter the name of the group, then select the users.
  1. Click the Next button.
  2. Review the summary page and complete any additional information requested on the page.
  3. Click Confirm Assignments.
Show App Embed Links

You can embed an Okta-managed app in a portal or other external location by obtaining the embed link.

  1. From the Dashboard, select Applications.
  2. Click the Show app embed links in the right sidebar. This displays the embed links for all of your Okta-managed applications.
  3. Copy the desired embed link URL and paste it into your portal or other external location outside of Okta.
  4. You can now sign into this app outside of Okta. To stop displaying the embed links, click the Hide app embed links button.

Note: Alternatively, you can obtain an embed link by selecting an application, selecting its General tab, and copying the URL provided in the Application Embed Link section.

Deactivate an App

This process only deactivates the app. It remains under the Inactive tab, unless it is permanently deleted, as is detailed in Deleting an AppAn abbreviation of application.   1. From the Dashboard, select Applications.
  2. Click the Active tab to find the app you wish to deactivate.
  3. From the Active drop-down menu button, choose Deactivate.
Delete an App

Caution: Deleting an app is a permanent state. Once deleted, it is not possible to re-activate the app or its memberships.

  1. From the Dashboard, select Applications.
  2. Click the Inactive tab. (If the app you wish to permanently delete is currently active, you are directed to deactivate it first).
  3. From the Inactive drop-down menu, choose Delete.
Customize an Application Logo

You can customize an application logo by replacing it with your own image. The custom logo must meet the following requirements:

  • Image type must be .png, .jpg, or, .gif (.png is recommended).
  • Image dimensions can't exceed 140 x 40 px.
  • Image size must be less than 25k.
  1. From the Dashboard, select Applications.
  2. Locate and click the app whose logo you want to customize.
  3. Mouse over the app's current logo, then click on the pencil icon.
  4. In the Edit Logo dialog box, use the Browse button to find the custom logo file, then click Update Logo.
  5. Click Close. The updated logo will be visible to all users who have been assigned this app.
  6. To revert to the original image, follow the same steps to access the Edit Logo dialog box, then click Reset logo.

Self Service Page

You can enable and manage applications so that your end users can add apps from their My Applications Home page.

Enable self service
  1. Select Applications > Self Service.
  2. On the Settings tab, select the Edit button in the Self Service Application Assignment section.
  3. Under Permissions, select the Enable self service application assignment check box and then choose which apps to show:
  • Show organization and end-user managed apps
  • Show organization managed apps only
  • Show end-user managed apps only
  1. Organization Managed Apps, select the apps that you want to appear in the organization tab of the self service app catalog and then click Save.
