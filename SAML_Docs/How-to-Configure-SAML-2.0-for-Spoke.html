How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Spoke

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Contact the Spoke Support team at support@unboxedtechnology.com and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account.

  2. Include the following information with your email:

    • IDP Issuer/Entity ID: Copy and paste the following:

      Sign in to the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

    • Login URL/SignOn URL: Copy and paste the following:

      Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

    • x.509 Certificate: Copy and paste the following (in PEM Format):

      Sign into the Okta Admin Dashboard to generate this variable.

  3. Spoke will process your request and will provide you a value for Company URL.

  4. In Okta, select the General tab for the Spoke org, then click Edit.

    • Enter the value Spoke provided you with in the Company URL field.

    • Click Save.

  5. Done!

    Notes:

    IdP-initiated flows and SP-initiated flows are supported.

    Just In Time (JIT) Provisioning is not supported.
