How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Assembla

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Login to Assembla as an Administrator.

  3. Navigate to the portfolio’s Admin tab (https://[yourSubDomain].assembla.com/p/admin)

  4. Scroll down to the SAML authentication section and check the Enable SAML box. This expands the SAML authentication section.

  5. Enter the following information (see screen shot at end of step for reference):

    • SAML Assertion Consumer Service URL: Copy and paste the following:

      Sign in to the Okta Admin app to generate this value.

    • X.509 Certificate: Copy and paste the following:

      Sign into the Okta Admin Dashboard to generate this variable.

    • Click Update SAML Settings.

  6. Done!

    Notes:

    IdP-initiated flows, SP-initiated flows and Just In Time (JIT) provisioning are all supported.

    For SP-initiated flows:

    1. Open your Assembla login URL: https://[yourSubDomain].assembla.com/p/home

    2. Click the Login with SAML button.
