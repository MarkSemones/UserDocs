How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Expensify

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Sign in to Expensify and navigate to Admin > Domain Control > [yourDomainName] > SAML to view the screen shown below.

  2. Enable SAML Login, as shown above.

  3. Enable Required for login, as shown above.

    Note: After choosing this options, users can only sign in with SSO and cannot use an Expensify password.

  4. Enter the following Identity Provider MetaData, as shown above.

  5. Done!

    Notes:

    IDP-initiated flows and SP-initiated flows are supported. Just In Time (JIT) provisioning is not supported.

    For an SP-initated flow go to https://www.expensify.com/signin and select SAML. On the next page, enter your email address and click the Go button.
