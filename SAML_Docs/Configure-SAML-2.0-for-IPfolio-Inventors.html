How to Configure SAML 2.0 for IPfolio - Inventors

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

 

  1. Contact the IPfolio Support team and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account.

  2. Attach the following IDP Metadata URL to your request:

    Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

  3. The IPfolio Support team will process your request and provide you with Login URL, Entity ID, Portal ID, Organization ID values.

  4. In Okta, select the General tab for the IPfolio app, then click Edit.

    • Enter the Login URL, Entity ID, Portal ID, Organization ID values into the corresponding fields.

    • Click Save.

  5. Done!

 

Notes:

IDP-initiated flows and Just In Time (JIT) Provisioning are supported.

SP-initiated flows is not supported.
