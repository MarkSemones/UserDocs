How to Configure SAML 2.0 for ACL Governance Risk Compliance

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Login to your ACL account as an administrator.

  2. Select Settings:

  3. In the Settings pane, under the Single Sign-On (SSO) Provider section, click on Manage SSO Settings:

  4. In the Single Sign-On (SSO) Provider page, enter the following (see screen shot at end of step for reference):
    • Custom domain: Make sure this value is the same as displayed in the Custom Domain field under the ADVANCED SIGN-ON SETTINGS from the Sign On tab of the ACL app in Okta (shown here):

    • Entity ID: Copy and paste the following into this field:

      Sign in to the Okta Admin app to generate this variable.

    • Redirect Login URL: Copy and paste the following into this field:

      Sign in to the Okta Admin app to generate this variable.

    • Security Certificate Fingerprint:

      • Sign in to the Okta Admin app to generate this variable.

    • Enable Single Sign-On (SSO): Check this box.
    • Click Save changes.

  5. Done!

    Notes:

    IdP-initiated flows, SP-initiated flows, and Just In Time (JIT) provisioning are all supported.

    For SP-initiated flows:

    • Go to your login URL.
    • Select Sign in to a custom domain:

    • Enter your domain name, then click CONTINUE:
