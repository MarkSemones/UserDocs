How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Epromo - E-Store

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

 

  1. Contact the Epromo Support team and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account.

  2. Include the following with your request:

    • IDP Metadata URL: Copy and paste the following:

      Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

    • Certificate: Download then attach the following certificate:

      Sign into the Okta Admin Dashboard to generate this variable.

  3. The Epromo Support team will process your request and will provide you with their SSO URL in the following format:

    https://store.epromo.com/saml_authenticate.php?acctid=[customerID]

  4. In Okta, select the General tab for the Epromo app, then click Edit.

    • Enter the Customer ID provided to you by Epromo ([customerID]) into the Customer ID field.

    • Click Save.

  5. Done!

 

Notes:

IDP-initiated flows and Just In Time (JIT) Provisioning are supported.

SP-initiated flows are not supported.
