How to Configure SAML 2.0 for SRXP

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

 

  1. Send an email to the SRXP Support team and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account.

  2. Save and attach the following file as metadata.xml and include it with your request:

    Sign in to the Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

  3. The SRXP Support team will process your request and provide you with a SSO URL in the following format: [yourBaseUrl]/#?pidp=[yourSlug].

  4. In Okta, select the General tab for the SRXP app, then click Edit.

    • Enter the Base URL and Slug values into the corresponding fields.

    • Click Save.

  5. Done!

 

Notes:

SP-initiated flows and Just In Time (JIT) Provisioning are supported

IDP-initiated flows are not supported.

For SP-initiated Flows

Open your SSO URL (provided by the SRXP Support team) in a browser.
