How to Configure SAML 2.0 for POPin

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

 

  1. Contact the POPin Support team and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account.

  2. Attach the following IDP Metadata URL to your request:

    Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

  3. The POPin Support team will process your request and provide you with their URL in the following format:

    https://[your-popin-subdomain].popinnow.com/saml/sso/[customer-id]

  4. In Okta, select the General tab for the POPin app, then click Edit.

    • Enter the URL provided to you by POPin: https://[your-popin-subdomain].popinnow.com/saml/sso/[customer-id] into the SSO URL field.

    • Click Save.

  5. Done!

 

Notes:

IDP-initiated flows, SP-initiated flows, and Just In Time (JIT) Provisioning are all supported.


For SP-initiated Flows

  1. Go to https://[your-popin-subdomain].popinnow.com/#/signin.

  2. Enter your email address.

  3. Click Submit.
