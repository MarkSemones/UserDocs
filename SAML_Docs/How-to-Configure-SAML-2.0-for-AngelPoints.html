How to Configure SAML 2.0 for AngelPoints

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

Contact AngelPoints' Customer Support

Ask AngelPoints' customer support to enable SAML 2.0 Single Sign-On for your account. They'll ask for the following information:

  1. External key:

    Sign in to the Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

  2. Public certificate:

    Sign in to the Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

Next steps

Wait for AngelPoints to send you further instructions on how to enable Single Sign-On for your organization.

You can assign AngelPoints access to your users at anytime from their user profile. In Administration, go to People to view and edit user profiles.
