Send an email to Xactly with your Okta Certificate

  1. Copy and paste the fields below to compose your own email:

    To

    support@xactlycorp.com

    Subject

    Please enable SAML 2.0 for <your organization name>

    Message

    Please enable SAML 2.0 access and create a shared key for company <your organization name>. The administrator for this account is <your administrator account>.
  2. Send your email
