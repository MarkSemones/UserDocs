How to Configure SAML 2.0 for YourCause

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Send an email to your YourCause Support team and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account.

  2. Include the following Metadata URL with your email:

    Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

  3. The YourCause support team will process your request. After receiving a confirmation email, you can start assigning people to the application.

  4. Done!

    Notes:

    IdP-initiated flows and SP-initiated flows are supported.

    Just in Time (JIT) provisioning is not supported.


For SP-initiated flows: Open your base URL as follows:

  • For the Production environment: Open https://[yourSubDomain].yourcause.com.

  • For the UAT environment: Open https://[yourSubDomain].yourcauseuat.com.
