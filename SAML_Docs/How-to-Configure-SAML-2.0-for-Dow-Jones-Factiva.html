How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Dow Jones Factiva

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Contact your Dow Jones Factiva relationship manager and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account. If you do not have contact details for a relationship manager, contact service@dowjones.com.

  2. Save the following as metadata.xml, and include it with your request.

    Sign in to Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

  3. Dow Jones Factiva will notify you when SAML is enabled and will provide you with an Account ID, Company name, and Product ID.

  4. In Okta, select the General tab for the Dow Jones Factiva app, then click Edit:

    • Enter the Account ID, Company name, and Product ID provided by Dow Jones Factiva.

    • Click Save.

  5. Done!


Notes:

  • SP-initiated flows, IDP-initiated flows, and Just In Time (JIT) provisioning are all supported.

  • For SP-initiated flows, use https://sso.dowjones.com/sp/startSSO.ping?PartnerIdpId=http://www.okta.com/[org.externalKey].
