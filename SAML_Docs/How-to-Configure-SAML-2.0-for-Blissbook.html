  2. Attach the following:

  4. Done!

    Notes:

    IdP-initiated flows and SP-initiated flows are asupported. Just In Time (JIT) provisioning is not supported.

    1. Open your base URL https://[yourSubDomain].blissbook.com/, where [yourSubDomain] is the name of your domain.
