This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

Contact Taleo to enable SAML 1.1 for your account

You will need to provide the following things to Taleo in order to complete the integration:

  1. Your Okta Identity Provider Certificate:

    Sign in to the Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

  2. An Issuer for the Certificate:

    Sign in to the Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

Next Steps

Once Taleo has activated SAML for your account, go to your Taleo instance in Okta and update your Taleo subdomain. Afterwards, Okta users assigned this Taleo instance can log in to Taleo with SAML.
