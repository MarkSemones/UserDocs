How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Circonus

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Log in to your Circonus account as an Administrator.

  2. Navigate to Account > Profile.

  3. Click EDIT SAML INTEGRATION.

  4. Enter the following (see screen shot at end of step for reference):

    • Email Domain: Enter your domain.

      Sign into the Okta Admin Dashboard to generate this variable.

    • Click SAVE.

  5. Done!


Notes:

SP-initiated flows, IDP-initiated flows, and Just In Time (JIT) provisioning are all supported.


For SP-initiated flows


  1. Go to: https://login.circonus.com.

  2. Enter your Email address (leave the Password field empty).

  3. Click the Okta SIGN IN button.
