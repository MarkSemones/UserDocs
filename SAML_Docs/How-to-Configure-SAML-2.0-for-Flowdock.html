How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Flowdock

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Contact the Flowdock support team and request that they enable SAML for your organization.

  2. Copy the following three items into the message to Flowdock support.

    1. Issuer:

      Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

    2. SAML URL:

      Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.

    3. Certificate Fingerprint:

      Sign into the Okta Admin dashboard to generate this value.
  3. Flowdock will process your request and will provide you a SAML URL in the format https://www.flowdock.com/auth/saml/callback?uid=aAAAAbbbbb1CdefgHiGklM where aAAAAbbbbb1CdefgHiGklM is the Customer Identifier.

  5. In Okta, on the General tab for the Flowdock app, select Edit.

  6. Enter the Customer Identifier, as shown below.

  8. Select Save.

  9. Done!

    Note: IdP-initiated flows, SP-initiated flows, and Just In Time (JIT) provisioning are all supported. For an SP-initiated flow, go to the sign in page at https://www.flowdock.com/login and click the LOG IN button.
