How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Adobe Sign

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

The following information is needed for configuring Adobe Sign

  1. Go to your Adobe Sign Account page, then under Account Settings, find the SAML settings section.

  2. For SAML Mode, choose either SAML Allowed or SAML Mandatory.
