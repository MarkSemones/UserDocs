This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  3. Trello will process your request and send you a confirmation email. They should also provide you with an Enterprise ID.

  4. In Okta, select the General for the Trello app.

  5. For Company Name, enter the Enterprise ID provided by Trello.

  6. Click Save.

  7. Done!

    Notes:
