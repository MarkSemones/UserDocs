How to Configure SAML 1.1 for Egencia

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Send an email to support@egencia.com

  2. The subject line should say "Enable inbound SSO for" your company

  3. The body of the email should include:

    1. Company name

    2. Email of the account administrator

    3. Redirect URL:

      In the Okta Admin Dashboard go to Application > Egencia > General > Application Embed Link to obtain this value.

    4. Portal URL:

      Sign in to the Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

  4. Download your Identity Provider Certificate

    Sign in to the Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

  5. Attach your Identity Provider Certificate to your email.

  6. Send your email and wait for further instructions from Egencia.

Next steps

Wait for Egencia to send you further instructions on how to enable Single Sign-On for your organization. If you want to allow your users to sign-on to Egencia immediately, you can change the sign-on mode to Secure Web Authentication. No further configuration will be required by you if you choose Secure Web Authentication.

You can assign Egencia access to your users at anytime from their user profile. Go to the People tab to view and edit user profiles.
