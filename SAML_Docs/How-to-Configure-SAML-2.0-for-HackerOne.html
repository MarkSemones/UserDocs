How to Configure SAML 2.0 for HackerOne

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Send an email to HackerOne Support (support@hackerone.com) and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your account.

  2. Attach the following information:

    • IDP Login URL: Copy and paste the following:

      Sign into the Okta Admin Dashboard to generate this variable.

    • x.509 Certificate (in .cert format): Certificate to download, then upload:

      Sign into the Okta Admin Dashboard to generate this variable.

  3. Done!

    Notes:

    IdP-initiated flows, SP-initiated flows, and Just In Time (JIT) provisioning are all supported.

    For SP-initiated flows:

    1. Go to https://hackerone.com/users/sign_in

    2. Enter your Email address.

    3. Click the Sign in button.
