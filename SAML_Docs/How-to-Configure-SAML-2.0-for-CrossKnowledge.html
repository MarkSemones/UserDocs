How to Configure SAML 2.0 for CrossKnowledge

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Send an email to the CrossKnowledge support team and request that they enable SAML. Attach the following IDP metadata:

  2. Attach the following values for the SAML attributes:
    • First name – firstname
    • Last name – lastname
    • E-mail – email
    • Login – key

  3. After receiving a confirmation email from CrossKnowledge, you can start assigning people to the application.

  4. Done!

Notes:

  • SP-initiated flows and Just in Time (JIT) provisioning are supported.
  • IdP-initiated flows are not supported.
  • For SP-initiated flows go to https://yourSubDomain.lms.crossknowledge.com/interfaces/login.php where yourSubDomain is your CrossKnowledge subdomain, and click the Connect with your corporate account button.
