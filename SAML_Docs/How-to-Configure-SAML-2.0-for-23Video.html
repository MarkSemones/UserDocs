How to Configure SAML 2.0 for 23 Video

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Copy the following IDP Metadata and save to a file named metadata.xml:

     
  2. Send an email to 23 Video Support and request that they enable SAML 2.0 for your organization.
  3. Attach the metadata.xml file you created in step 1 to the email message.
  4. 23 Video will process your application. After receiving a confirmation email, you can assign people to the application.

  5. Done!

    Notes:

    IdP-initiated flows and SP-initiated flows are supported. Just In Time (JIT) provisioning is not supported.

    For an SP-initiated flow, go to https://<yourBaseURL>/saml/redirect, where <yourBaseURL> is your 23 Video custom domain.
