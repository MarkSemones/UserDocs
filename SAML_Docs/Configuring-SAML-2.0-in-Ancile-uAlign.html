How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Ancile uAlign

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Log in to you Ancile uAlign Admin console, go to the Settings gear on the upper right corner and click on Organization.

  2. Next, click on the Edit icon on the right side as shown below.

  3. Scroll down to the Authentication Settings and check Enable SAML 2.0 Authentication.
