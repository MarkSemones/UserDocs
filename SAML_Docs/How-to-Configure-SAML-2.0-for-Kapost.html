How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Kapost

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Login to Kapost as an administrator, then navigate to Settings > Single Sign On.

  2. Check the Enable Sign Sign-On box.

  3. Make a copy of the values from the SAML Consumer URL and SAML Metadata URL fields.

  4. In Okta, select the General tab for the Kapost app and enter the values you just saved into the corresponding fields, as shown below:

  5. Click Save.

  6. In Kapost SSO Settings section, enter the following (see screen shot at end of step for reference):

    IdP-initiated flows, SP-initiated flows, and Just In Time (JIT) provisioning are all supported.
