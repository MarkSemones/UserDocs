How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Saba

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Sign in to Saba support at https://support.saba.com and open a new support ticket. Ask them to enable SAML 2.0 Single Sign-On for your account and provide the following information in the service request:

    IDP metadata:

    Sign in to Okta Admin app to have this variable generated for you.

  2. After the request is completed, verify that you can log in through your IDP endpoint.

  3. Provide your users with the redirect URL for your users to use to sign in.

  4. Saba Personal Cloud supports SP-initiated SSO. To simulate an IDP-initiated flow, use the Okta Bookmark app. For more information, see Simulating an IDP-initiated Flow with the Bookmark App. The URL to use is similar to https://<environment>.sabacloud.com/Saba/Web/SC3 where can be ns1 or prodqa.

  5. Done!
