Before you begin

Read this before you enable SAML

Rally supports hybrid SSO, as well as SSO-only mode. If you use SSO-only mode, it will impact all of your users. It is recommended that you ask Rally support to configure hybrid SSO while you are testing the integration. Once testing is completed to your satisfaction, you can contact Rally support to switch to SSO-only mode if you opt to do so.
