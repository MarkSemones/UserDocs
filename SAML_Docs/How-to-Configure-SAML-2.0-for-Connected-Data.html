How to Configure SAML 2.0 for Connected Data

This setup might fail without parameter values that are customized for your organization. Please use the Okta Administrator Dashboard to add an application and view the values that are specific for your organization.

  1. Log in to the Transporter Management website as your Organization Admin.

  2. Navigate to Organization Settings.

  3. Click on the Manage IdP Integration button:

  4. The Identity Provider Integration window opens, enter the following (see screen shot at end of step for reference):

    • Click the Save Identity Provider Settings button.

  5. Wait until you receive a IdP settings were successfully saved. Paste the information below into your IdP message.

  6. Make a copy of the value from the SAML Consumer URL field, then click the Close button:

  7. In Okta, select the General tab for the Connected Data app, then click Edit.

    • Enter the SAML Consumer URL value you saved earlier into the corresponding field.

    • Click Save:

  8. Done!

    Notes:

    SP-initiated flows and IdP-initiated flows are supported.

    Just In Time (JIT) provisioning is not supported.
